Perl Certification & Rank & Degree Requirements
Last Updated 20161121 2016.326


LEVEL 1 CERTIFICATION COURSES:

Students in each certification course (1A, 1B, etc) must pass a formal examination.
Graduates of each course will receive a framed certificate & 1 year of Perl career mentorship.
A minimum of 5 participants are required for each course.
Students must provide a laptop which can be totally wiped clean for fresh Perl installation.
All 3 course textbooks are utilized throughout level 1 certification & are included in course fees.

LEVEL 1A, PERL WHITE BELT:
    Go from zero to Perl & earn your white belt by getting Perl installed on your own laptop & writing your first Perl programs.
    Topics: Linux & Perl Installation, Scalar Data, Array Data, Subroutines
    Chapters 1 - 4
    24 credit hours
    $650 per student

LEVEL 1B, PERL YELLOW BELT:
    Earn your second rank belt by writing more complex Perl programs & increasing your confidence as an aspiring software developer.
    Topics: File Operations, Hash Data, Regular Expressions, Additional Control Structures
    Chapters 5 - 10
    40 credit hours
    $950 per student

LEVEL 1C, PERL ORANGE BELT:
    Earn your full level 1 certification & advance to the rank of orange belt by completing all textbook exercises & launching a real-life web app.
    Topics: Object-Oriented Programming, Operating System Processes, Dynamic Web Development, Additional Advanced Techniques
    Real-Life Web Application Project & Follow-Up Sessions
    Chapters 11 - 17
    56 credit hours
    $1,250 per student

LEVEL 1 BUNDLE, LEVELS 1A + 1B + 1C:
    Save money & earn all 3 rank belts for full level 1 certification!
    $350 discount
    $2,500 per student
